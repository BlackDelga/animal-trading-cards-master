My Favor Animal is Lion

African Lion is bigger than the Asiatic Lion and it's the second largest big cat, after the Tiger.

  • Wild: Nearly all wild Lions live in Africa
  • Average Weigh: On average, males weigh 190kg(almost 30 stone)
  • Hunt: Lions do most of their hunting at night
  • Threat: The main threat are retaliatory

Lions are one of Africa's most recognizable animals and for good reason: more than any other animal in the African wilderness, lions signify courage, strength, and power. Spending up to 20 hours of the day sleeping or resting, lions are the laziest of the big cats.
